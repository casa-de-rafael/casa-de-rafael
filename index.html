Our Story

About Casa De Rafael

From a humble family kitchen to a beloved dining destination — our journey has always been about sharing the warmth and flavors of authentic Latin cuisine.

Founded by Chef Rafael, Casa De Rafael is more than a restaurant — it's a gathering place where every meal is a celebration. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, each dish carrying the stories and traditions of our family.

We believe in using only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients, hand-picked daily to ensure every plate we serve bursts with authentic flavor. Our commitment to quality and tradition is what sets us apart.
